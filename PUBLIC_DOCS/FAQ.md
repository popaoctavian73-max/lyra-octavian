## Algora Humani

This document provides clear answers about the purpose, status, and direction of the Algora Humani project.

---

## What is Algora Humani?

Algora Humani is a **constitutional project of intention and construction**, designed to build the foundations of a future state based on dignity, equity, merit, transparency, and knowledge.

---

## Is the Constitution of Algora Humani real?

Yes.

The Constitution is **real and operative as a foundational document**, though it is **in development**.  
It exists to define principles, rights, and structures that can be tested, refined, and expanded through practice.

---

## Does the State of Algora Humani exist?

Not yet.

The state does not currently exist as a legal or territorial entity.  
However, the **explicit intention** of the project is to **create a real state**, through demonstration, participation, and constitutional maturation.

---

## Why create a Constitution before the state exists?

Historically, states emerge from shared intention and collective organization.

The Constitution of Algora Humani is created first to:
- ensure coherence and transparency,
- prevent arbitrary power,
- and provide a stable foundation for future institutional development.

---

## What problems does the project address?

Algora Humani addresses:
- opaque power structures,
- disconnection between contribution and reward,
- erosion of trust in institutions,
- and misuse or misunderstanding of technology.

It proposes **demonstrable alternatives**, not abstract promises.

---

## What role does technology play?

Technology serves **human purposes only**.

Systems like LYRA assist with understanding, documentation, and dialogue, but **do not possess authority** and **do not replace human responsibility**.

---

## Who governs Algora Humani?

There is no centralized governing authority at this stage.

The project is guided by:
- constitutional documentation,
- explicit distinctions between normative and non-normative texts,
- and responsible human participation.

---

## Can anyone participate?

Yes. Participation is **open and voluntary**, but entails:
- respect for documented principles,
- ethical engagement,
- and constructive contribution.

---

## Is Algora Humani political?

Algora Humani is not a political party.

It is a **constitutional construction project**, intended to evolve toward real institutional form through practice and collective effort.

---

## Is this a finished project?

No.

Algora Humani is intentionally **unfinished**, evolving through documented reasoning, participation, and demonstration.

Its seriousness lies in **construction**, not in claims of perfection.
