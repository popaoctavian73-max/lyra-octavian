Algora Humani is a **constitutional project of intention and construction**, aimed at building the foundations of a future state through documented reasoning, participation, and practical demonstration.

This index is **evolutionary** and will be updated as documents are added, revised, or refined.

## Normative Status of Documents

- **Normative documents**  
  Establish foundational principles, rights, and structural rules.

- **Explanatory documents**  
  Clarify meaning and intent without altering norms.

- **Exploratory documents**  
  Examine possibilities, scenarios, and open questions related to construction and implementation.

Readers are encouraged to respect these distinctions when engaging with the materials.

---

## Access, Use, and Responsibility

All public documents are:
- openly accessible,
- read-only,
- provided for study, critique, and responsible participation.

They do not yet constitute external legal authority, but serve as the **constitutional basis for a state in construction**.
